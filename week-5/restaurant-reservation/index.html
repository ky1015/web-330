Welcome to the Omatikaya Forest Cafe!

Based on Pandora: the world of Avatar, this dining experience will transport you to a different planet. You can choose to dine in the Forest Room, the Cave Room, or the Ikran Rookery.

Tables 1-5 are in the Forest Room, tables 6-10 are in the Cave Room, and tables 11-15 are in the Ikran Rookery. To reserve a table, please enter your name and preferred table number, as well as a time from 1-12.
